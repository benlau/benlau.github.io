What is AutoStudio?

AutoStudio is a novel framework designed to address the challenges of multi-turn interactive image generation, a task requiring models to interact with users over multiple rounds to create a sequence of coherent images

What could AutoStudio do?

AutoStudio can be used for several applications, including multi-turn editing, open-ended story and manga book generation
