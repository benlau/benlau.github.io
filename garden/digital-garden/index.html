Digital Garden

Created Oct 3, 2022 - Last updated: Apr 25, 2024

Seeding 🌱
  • 一種以分享知識為目的的個人網站
  • 跟BLOG不同，主體並非按時間排序的文章，Digital Garden更像是持續更新的Wiki，文章之間以連結聯繫起來。
  • 由 Evergreen Note/長春筆記 衍生出來的概念
  • 分享長春筆記，就像修整自己的花園一樣，持續更新並完善概念
  • 長春筆記的特點：
    • 每篇筆記只寫一件事(Atomic)
    • 筆記之間有緊密的連結
    • 為自己而寫，並非為讀者而寫
    • 放棄層階性的架構分類，用網絡連接概念

所以

  • 即使發佈未完成的內容也不用良心不安（無誤）
  • 寫廢文吧，讓廢文有一個歸宿
  • 寫錯字也沒問題（無誤？）
  • 胡言亂語⋯⋯不要，小心被炎上

A Brief History & Ethos of the Digital Garden

My small collection highlighted a number of sites that are taking a new approach to the way we publish personal knowledge on the web.

They’re not following the conventions of the “personal blog,” as we’ve come to know it. Rather than presenting a set of polished articles, displayed in reverse chronological order, these sites act more like free form, work-in-progress wikis.

A garden is a collection of evolving ideas that aren’t strictly organised by their publication date. They’re inherently exploratory – notes are linked through contextual associations.

Digital Gardening - Ness Labs

Digital gardening consists in cultivating a mind garden by consistently improving on interconnected evergreen notes. Digital gardeners seed their garden with quality content, grow their knowledge by forming new branches and connecting the dots, and share the fruits of their thought with the world.
