About Ben Lau

A Open Source Developer from Hong Kong

I am a conceptor, maker, and full-stack developer who keeps building open-source projects and thinks that the purpose of life is to create.

This site is my digital garden, a place to share and grow idea. You may find my working projects and evergreen notes here. The content come from my notes in Joplin. I write a Joplin Web Export Tool to convert to Hugo (Static Site Generator). I hope you will find some interesting stuff here.

This site is powered by Joplin, Hugo and Hugo Theme for digital gardening projects.
